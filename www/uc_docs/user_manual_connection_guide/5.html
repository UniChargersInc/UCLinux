RSII in dBm Signal Strength 50 Excellent 60 Very Good 70 Good 80 Low 90 Very Low 0 No Signal The RSSI must be between 50-79 to update the charger and use smart features. If the RSSI reading is above 80 install a Wi-Fi repeater to increase the signal strength or move the Wi-Fi router closer to the charger. Note: The Grizzl-E Smart does not support 5Gz frequency. Ensure your router uses 2.4 Ghz frequency.
