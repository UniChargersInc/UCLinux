	<h2>Smart Charging</h2>

		<button class="accordion">Charge and Get Report</button>
			<div class="panel">
				<ol>
					<li>Insert the charging Connector into the EV and ensure the connector is fully seated/locked in place. Once complete, the charging session will begin.</li>
					<li>Ensure the Charging Status shows charging.</li>
					<li>When the charging session is finished remove the charging connector and return to the holster.</li>
					<li>Go to the Reports tab.</li>
					<li>Select the charging station from the dropdown menu.</li>
					<li>Select the date range for the reports.</li>
					<li>Select Get Report.</li>
					<li>Report will display Amperage (A) and Kilowatts (kW) over time (minutes).</li>
				</ol>
				<img src="Report_Graph.png" Alt="Report Graph">
			</div>
		
		<button class="accordion">Set Charging Schedule</button>
			<div class="panel">
				<ol>
					<li>Go to the Charging Profile tab. Ensure the correct Charger Serial number displays at the top of the screen.</li>
					<li>Set the correct time zone and daylight saving setting. Select the Update button.</li>
					<li>Select the Add Profile button from the top menu.</li>
					<li>Set the Valid From and Valid To dates.</li>
					<li>Select the date and time for the Start of the Schedule. The vehicle will start charging at the time selected.</li>
					<li>Set the Charge Limit to the Maximum Current or Power. For example, if you want to charge at 32A for the first time period, type 32 into the Charge Limit field.</li>
					<li>Set the Duration for the number of hours you want to charge at the Charge Limit. For example, if you wanted to charge at 32A for 2 hours, type 2 in the Duration field.</li>
					<li>Select the Add New Period. The Duration (minutes) and Charging Current (A) will be graphed on the profile screen.</li>
					<li>Add additional Charging Periods as desired. Set the Charge Limit and Duration for the next period. Amperage and Duration will be graphed on the profile screen.
						<img src="Charging_Profile.png" Alt="Report Graph"></li>
					<li>Select the Save Profile button from the top menu.</li>
					<li>Refresh the page and login to acharger.ca.</li>
					<li>Ensure the profile number shows in the top menu.</li>
				</ol>
			</div>
	
		<button class="accordion">Change Charging Profile</button>
			<div class="panel">
				<p>If you want to change the time period, duration, or current settings you will need to create a new profile:</p>
				<ol>
					<li>Select <b>Clear Profile</b> button in the top menu. Ensure the message <i>No Profile found for station</i> shows on screen.</li>
					<li>Select <b>Add Profile.</b></li>
					<li>Set the <i>StartSchedule</i> for the date and time you want the profile to start.</li>
					<li>Set the Charge Limit and Duration.</li>
					<li>Select the <b>Add Period</b> button. A line should be graphed on the Profile.</li>
					<li>Select <b>Save Profile</b>.</li>
					<li>Refresh the page.</li>
					<li>Login and select the Profile from the top drop down box.</li>
				</ol>
			</div>
